MAGNUS

Overview

The Magnus is a powerful and versatile Tenno revolver. The Magnus works as a general-purpose version of the Sicarus revolver, as it primarily deals Impact damage, while it deals such damage on semi-automatic. It also comes with higher Slash and Puncture values, plus a good status chance. The sacrificed characteristics are DPS, as the gun is not a burst-fire and it has a smaller magazine capacity.
